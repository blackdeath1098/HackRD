About Us!

Cultur3ducation was created by Phoenyx Ali, Ezra Yun, Ashley Toribio, and Britney Bowman. They are students part of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program located in Brooklyn, NY. All of the girls are still in high school and excited to see how Cultur3ducation will change society's perception on others. :)

Every week a country will be "showcased" and everyday the website will automatically update to reveal an aspect of that country. The users can learn about the country and something new eveyday!

Why This, Not That?

Everyday a few articles are linked to the page for further information on the given topic. They've been purposefully handpicked by the makers of Cultur3ducation. Every article is looked through to check for legitimacy and creativity. We, at Cultur3ducation, believe that the users of this website shouldn't have to go through tons and tons of writing to learn about something, they should be able to get a basic idea and have a source where it will provide more information on given topic. We look from your point of view and try to choose the resources that we believe the users want to see and use. Any suggestions are welcome, just email us at cultur3ducation@gmail.com!
